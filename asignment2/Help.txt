User guide for RFID Reader.

To use:
1) Ensure that your RFID reader is connected to a supported USB port.
2) Click the connect button at the top of the program.
3) Wait for the confirmation that you have been connected.
4) Once connected:
	a) Place a tag over the RFID reader to allow the program to read
	   the tag.
5) Click disconnect when you are done reading RFID tags.
